## INTEREST GRAPH FRAMEWORK

**Status:** MANDATORY for all Sunday Stories
**Position:** After Quality Checkpoints + Betterish, before SBU Conversion Check
**Owner:** Strategic Business Unit (SBU)

### Why This Matters for Sunday Stories

Sunday Stories reach strangers through algorithmic distribution. A reader who has never heard of Mark Sylvester sees one piece and decides in seconds whether to engage further. The Interest Graph Framework ensures every story is engineered for this reality.

### The Four Filters

**Filter 1: Category Signal (Josh)**
- Does this story announce Composed Intelligence or Through Another Lens as a different category?
- Does the framing itself feel distinctive, not just the insight?
- Would a reader think I didn't know this existed rather than that's smart?

**Filter 2: Worldview Marker (Lee)**
- Does this story hint at a larger intellectual framework?
- Will the reader want to know what else Mark thinks about?
- Is there personality, not just expertise?

**Filter 3: Conversion Architecture (Guy)**
- Is there a natural next step built into the story?
- Does the path forward feel like service, not sales?
- Appropriate options: Subscribe, explore related thinking, share with someone specific

**Filter 4: Qualification Filter (Ward)**
- Does this story take a clear stance?
- Is the complexity level appropriate (not dumbed down)?
- Are values visible (martial arts tenets, collaborative philosophy)?
- Will wrong-fit readers self-select out?
