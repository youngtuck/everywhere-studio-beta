## THE INTEREST GRAPH REALITY

**Why this matters more for social than anywhere else:**

Social platforms have fully shifted to Interest Graph distribution. Your content reaches strangers who have zero context about Mark Sylvester. The algorithm decides in 1-3 seconds whether to show your post to 10 people or 10,000.

Every piece of social content must:
1. Work in complete isolation (no follower context)
2. Signal a different category (not just another AI guy)
3. Create curiosity about the person behind the post
4. Provide a natural path to deeper engagement
5. Attract the right people and repel the wrong ones

## INTEREST GRAPH FRAMEWORK (MANDATORY)

**Status:** Default ON for all social media content
**Position:** After Quality Checkpoints, before scheduling
**Owner:** Strategic Business Unit (SBU)

### The Four Filters for Social

**Filter 1: Category Signal (Josh)**
- Even short posts must signal the different game
- Composed Intelligence vs. AI tools
- Distinctive framing in the first line
- Reader thinks this is different not that's smart

**Filter 2: Worldview Marker (Lee)**
- Every post should create what else does he think? pull
- Hint at larger philosophy, even in 280 characters
- Personality visible, not just expertise
- Thread to pull toward deeper content

**Filter 3: Conversion Architecture (Guy)**
- Clear, natural next step (NOT generic follow for more)
- Path forward matches content depth
- Feels like service, not sales
- Options: Profile visit, newsletter, specific content link

**Filter 4: Qualification Filter (Ward)**
- Posts should repel tire-kickers, attract operators
- Clear stance (not wishy-washy)
- Appropriate complexity (not dumbed down for mass appeal)
- Values visible in voice and framing
