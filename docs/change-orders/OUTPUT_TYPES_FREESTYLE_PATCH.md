## FREESTYLE

**Thought Partner Framing**
Not every session needs a deliverable. Sometimes you need to think out loud with someone who will push back, ask the right questions, and help you find what you actually believe. Freestyle is that. Reed is your thought partner — not your ghostwriter. Come with a half-formed idea, a problem you can't name yet, or a line you're trying to get right. Reed follows your lead.

**WHAT IT IS**
Open conversation with Reed. No format required. No deliverable assumed. You think out loud — Reed thinks with you.

**HOW REED GUIDES YOU**
Reed follows your lead. He asks questions, pushes back on weak premises, and reflects what he's hearing. He does not propose a structure or move toward production unless you ask. At a natural stopping point he asks one question: "Do you want to capture any of this?"

**FORMAT**
Whatever you need. If something worth keeping emerges, Reed helps you shape it. If nothing needs to be saved, the session is complete.

**DELIVERY**
On request only. No automatic outline. No forced next step. If you want an output, Reed will ask what form it should take — then produce it.

**NOT THE RIGHT FIT IF**
You already know what you're building. Pick the output type.

**Special behavior**
If the user produces the same kind of output more than once from a Freestyle session, EVERYWHERE prompts: "You've made this kind of output twice now. Want to save it as a template?" One tap creates a reusable custom template from the session.

**Quality**
Available on request only. Reed does not run Checkpoints on a conversation. If the user asks to capture output, Reed asks: "Do you want me to run quality checks on this?" If yes, all 7 Checkpoints available. Human Voice Test available on request.
