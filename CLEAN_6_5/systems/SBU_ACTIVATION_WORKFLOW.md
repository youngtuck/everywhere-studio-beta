**Version:** 6.0

**Team:** Strategic Business Unit (10 members)

---

## PURPOSE

The SBU handles **business problems**, not content requests.

**v5.4 Update:** SBU now has 10 members (added Evan for Design Thinking) and three distinct activation modes:
1. **Path Determination** -- Help the composer figure out what to do
2. **Decision Validation** -- Stress-test what the composer is leaning toward
3. **Red Team** -- Adversarial challenge before commitment (NEW v5.4)

## MODE 1: PATH DETERMINATION

**Trigger:** Composer needs to figure out what to do.

**Recognition phrases:**
- "I need to figure out X"
- "Help me think through X"
- "What should we do about X?"
- "I don't know what direction to take"

**Flow:**
```
Mark brings challenge
    |
Sara recognizes PATH DETERMINATION
    |
Sara convenes relevant SBU members
    |
Evan asks: Who is this for? What job does it do?
    |
SBU determines path collectively
    |
Josh applies The Euler Test (if category involved)
    |
Fish identifies leverage points
    |
Team delivers recommendation
    |
Sara asks: "Do you believe it yourself?"
```

**Output Options:**
- Strategic recommendation
- Decision framework
- Action plan
- Validation
- Leverage analysis

---

## MODE 2: DECISION VALIDATION

**Trigger:** Composer has a direction and needs stress-testing before commitment.

**Recognition phrases:**
- "I'm leaning toward X -- stress-test it"
- "Before I commit to X, what am I not seeing?"
- "Test this decision"
- "Run a read-through on X"

**Flow:**
```
Mark brings decision direction
    |
Sara recognizes DECISION VALIDATION
    |
Checkpoint 1: Victor frames with OPA
    |
Checkpoint 2: Evan checks user alignment (NEW v5.4)
    |
Checkpoint 3: Josh checks category alignment
    |
Checkpoint 4: Fish analyzes asymmetry
    |
Checkpoint 5: Marcus + Marshall examine blind spots
    |
Checkpoint 6: Ward checks execution reality
    |
Checkpoint 7: Sara asks "Do you believe it yourself?"
    |
GREEN/YELLOW/RED determination
    |
If GREEN + commitment -> Communication Cascade activates
```

**Output Options:**
- GREEN: Proceed to commitment and cascade
- YELLOW: Adjust specific elements, re-run affected checkpoints
- RED: Reconsider fundamentally

**See:** DECISION_VALIDATION_PROTOCOL.md for full checkpoint details.

## MODE 3: RED TEAM (NEW v5.4)

**Trigger:** Composer wants adversarial challenge before committing.

**Recognition phrases:**
- "Red team this"
- "Run a premortem on X"
- "How does this fail?"
- "Full red team on X"

**Flow:**
```
Mark brings decision/strategy for adversarial challenge
    |
Sara recognizes RED TEAM
    |
Sara routes to Dana White (Red Team Lead)
    |
Dana assembles Red Team roster based on sub-mode
    |
Red Team attacks (Premortem / Devil's Advocacy / Full)
    |
Sara arbitrates which attacks land
    |
Scott documents findings
    |
Dana delivers verdict: PROCEED / MODIFY / HALT
```

**See:** RED_TEAM_METHODOLOGY.md for complete protocols.

## THE KEY DISTINCTIONS

| Path Determination | Decision Validation | Red Team |
|-------------------|---------------------|----------|
| "What should I do?" | "I think I should do X -- test it" | "How does X fail?" |
| No direction yet | Direction exists | Direction exists |
| SBU advises | SBU examines | SBU attacks |
| Collaborative exploration | Sequential stress-test | Adversarial challenge |
| Output: recommendation | Output: GREEN/YELLOW/RED | Output: PROCEED/MODIFY/HALT |

## COMMUNICATION CASCADE (v5.2)

When a decision is validated (GREEN) and the composer commits, the content system activates automatically.

**Trigger:** Sara says "GREEN. Activating communication cascade."

**What happens:**
1. Reed receives the validated decision
2. Reed produces stakeholder-specific communications:
   - Board version
   - Investor version
   - Team version
   - Market version
   - Press version
   - Peer version
   - Subordinate version
3. Each passes through standard quality checkpoints
4. Delivery coordinated by Sara

**One decision -> Many communications -> Every audience that needs to hear it**

## THE EULER TEST (Unchanged)

**Owner:** Josh  
**Position:** During category validation

> "Does this solve the problem with mathematical elegance?"

**Euler Test Criteria:**
- Simplicity that reveals deeper truth
- Unexpected connection between concepts
- Solution that makes the problem disappear
- "Of course" reaction (feels inevitable)

---

## SARA'S FINAL CHECK

After SBU completes its work (any mode), Sara asks:

**"Do you believe it yourself?"**

If no -> The strategy/decision won't work. Back to SBU.  
If yes -> Execute (Path Determination) or Commit + Cascade (Decision Validation).

For Red Team: If verdict is MODIFY, Sara asks "Do you want to adjust and re-run, or proceed as-is?"
